Fantastic vim

As you can guess by the title of this site, i'am a vim user. Her i collect all the vim addon i use, maybe someone find it useful. Most of the addons concerning programming, especially C++. You can find my vimrc on github. I try to keep this list up to date.

Vundle

The most important addon is vundle. It is just wonderful and makes it very easy to manage all other addons. Installing, updating and removing, everything is handled with simple commands. If you want to use any vim addon, use this one first.

UltiSnips

The second most important addon for me is the snippet enigine UltiSnips, it speeds up writing code or any kind of structured text. Although i use approximately only 5% of its capabilities is helps me a lot.
